About us

Our main goal here at Planendar is to help users navigate through their day to day life while being able to keep track of their goals and what they they need to achieve. In the end, we hope to help users find jobs by giving them the resources they need to succeed.

Features

  • Stores user information
  • Resume building tips provided
  • Jobs recommended to user based on student attributes and location
  • Planner with pre-generated deadlines based on input from user
